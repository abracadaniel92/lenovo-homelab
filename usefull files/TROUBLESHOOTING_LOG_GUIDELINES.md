## Best Practices

1. **Log Immediately**: Write entries while the work is fresh in your mind
2. **Be Detailed**: Include enough context that you'll understand it 6 months later
3. **Include Commands**: Actual commands run are valuable for future reference
4. **Document Mistakes**: Failed approaches are learning opportunities
5. **Success Stories**: Document what worked well, not just what broke
6. **Cross-Reference**: Link to related issues or documentation

## Remember

> "The best time to document is now. Your future self will thank you."

This log is a **knowledge base** - treat it as a valuable resource for understanding your infrastructure's evolution and learning from past experiences.
